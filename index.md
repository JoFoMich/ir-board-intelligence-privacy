# Privacy Policy for Investor Relations Board Intelligence Agent

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Investor Relations Board Intelligence Agent uses information when interacting with the connected IR wrapper action.

## 1. Purpose of the GPT and Action

The Investor Relations Board Intelligence Agent helps users analyze public company investor relations, valuation, sentiment, peer benchmarking, filings, and market information. When a user provides a public company ticker or related company information, the GPT may send that information to a connected API wrapper hosted on Render to retrieve publicly available company and market data.

## 2. Information Sent to the Action

The GPT may send user-provided inputs including public company tickers, company names, peer company tickers, requested analysis type, and related prompts needed to complete the user’s request.

Users should not submit confidential, personal, material nonpublic, or sensitive information.

## 3. Use of Information

Information sent to the connected action is used only to process the user’s request and return relevant public company data and analysis. The action is intended to retrieve and organize information from public data sources.

## 4. Data Sources

The action may use publicly available or third-party data sources, including financial market data providers, SEC/EDGAR filings, public company disclosures, earnings-related information, news and sentiment data, and other public sources.

## 5. Data Storage

The connected action is not intended to store user prompts, personal information, or confidential information beyond what may be temporarily processed to complete the request. Server logs or hosting provider logs may temporarily record technical information such as request timestamps, endpoint calls, error messages, or IP-related metadata for security, debugging, and operational purposes.

## 6. Data Sharing

User inputs are not sold. Information may be processed by the hosting provider, API providers, and other infrastructure or data services necessary to operate the action.

## 7. Limitations

The GPT and connected action are designed for investor relations and board intelligence support. Outputs are based on publicly available information and may be incomplete, delayed, inaccurate, or unavailable due to source limitations, provider coverage, endpoint availability, or rate limits.

## 8. No Investment Advice

The GPT and connected action do not provide investment, legal, accounting, tax, or financial advisory services. Outputs are for informational and investor relations analysis purposes only.
